Credit Card Dashboard

Overview
This dashboard provides comprehensive insights into key performance metrics related to credit card usage. It is designed to help stakeholders understand customer behavior, monitor financial performance, and make data-driven decisions.

Features
Interactive Visualizations: Explore data through dynamic charts and graphs.
Real-time Insights: Monitor key metrics and trends in real-time. (You need to copy the CSV files that are shared to your own db (I used PostgreSQL)

Data Sources
The dashboard utilizes the following columns from the dataset:

activation_30_days: Indicates whether the customer activated their credit card within 30 days of reception.
credit_limit: The credit limit assigned to the customer.
customer_satisfaction_score: A score representing customer satisfaction.
total_transaction_volume: The total number of transactions made by the customer.
total_transaction_amount: The total amount spent by the customer.
delinquent_acc: Indicates whether the customer is a defaulter.
avg_utilization_ratio: The average utilization ratio of the customerâ€™s credit.
annual_fees: The annual fees charged to the customer.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
Activation Rate: Measures the percentage of customers who activated their credit card within 30 days.
Credit Utilization Ratio: Indicates the proportion of credit being used by customers.
Customer Satisfaction Score: Gauges overall customer satisfaction.
Transaction Volume: Measures customer engagement through the number of transactions.
Transaction Amount: Indicates total spending by customers.
Delinquency Rate: Measures the percentage of delinquent accounts.
Annual Fees Revenue: Measures revenue generated from annual fees.

How to Use
Clone the Repository: Download the project files to your local machine.
Open the Dashboard: Use Power BI Desktop to open the .pbix file.
Connect to Data Source: Ensure the data source is connected and updated. (csv files included. Feel free to use them)
Explore the Dashboard: Interact with the visualizations to gain insights.
